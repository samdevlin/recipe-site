# Basmati Rice

A super easy recipe for making some basmati rice for the week.

![rice innit](https://www.indianveggiedelight.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/03/instant-pot-basmati-rice.jpg)

## Ingredients

- Basmati (or any long grain rice)
- Water

## Method

1. Add the desired amount of rice to the pot. **Don't wash it!**
2. Add 1.5x the amount of water to rice in the pot.
3. Mix and put on medium high heat with the lid off.
4. Once the entire surface is bubbly, put the lid on, reduce the heat to medium low and cook for 12 mins.
5. After 12 mins, remove from the heat and rest for 10 mins.
6. Fluff!
